# Getting Started

Welcome to Daily Life Assistant! This guide will help you get started with the app.

## Overview

Daily Life Assistant helps you manage your finances, schedule, and daily life activities in one place.

## First Steps

1. **Set up your profile**
   - Go to Settings
   - Configure your language and currency preferences

2. **Add your first income**
   - Navigate to Finance > Recurring Income
   - Add your regular income sources

3. **Track your expenses**
   - Use Daily Expenses to record your spending
   - Set up Recurring Expenses for fixed costs

## Key Features

- **Financial Management**: Track income, expenses, budgets, and savings
- **Schedule Management**: Plan your daily activities and special occasions
- **Reports**: View detailed financial reports and insights

## Need Help?

If you need assistance, you can:
- Contact us via Messenger or Email from Settings
- Browse more guides in the User Guide section

Happy managing!
